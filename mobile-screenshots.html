How to Capture Actual Screenshots

  1. Open Chrome DevTools with F12 or Cmd+Option+I
  2. Click the device toggle icon or press Cmd+Shift+M
  3. Select a device preset (iPhone 14 Pro, Pixel 7, etc.)
  4. Navigate through the app to each screen state
  5. Capture with Cmd+Shift+P then "Capture screenshot"
  6. Save images to readme-assets/ folder
